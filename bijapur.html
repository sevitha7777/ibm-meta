Bijapur, renamed as Vijapura in 2021, is a cultural hub and has plenty of mosques, mausoleums, palaces, and forts as offerings to travelers. Bijapur is one of the popular tourist spots in Karnataka and a heart for the Lingayat brand of Shaivism. The Lingayat Siddeshwara Festival is celebrated with full splendor for eight days in January and February here. Do not miss these when visited, Go Sightseeing, Shopping, Click pictures, Explore the culture and living of the Islamic dynasty that existed there. The ideal duration can be one to two days. The best time to visit the place is October to February. Sambre Airport is located at 164 km in Belgaum and Bijapur has its own Railway Station.
