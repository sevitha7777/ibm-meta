Mysore

Mysore is one of the largest princely states that boats numerous tourist spots for you to get in touch with the royal history of this place. Also known as the city of palaces in southern India, it is the most idyllic destination to visit in Karnataka with a dash of royal touch. The city not just houses world-renowned palaces and landmarks but there are also gleaming beaches near Mysore that would surely leave you amazed. Mysore is also famous for its silk sarees, the intricate architecture, sandalwood, the royal heritage and yoga. However, the biggest attraction of the city is the Mysore Palace that reflects the beauty and charm of the royal city.
