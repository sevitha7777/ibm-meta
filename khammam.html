Khammam

Considered to be one of the best tourist spots of Telangana, this town is located at a distance of 198 km from Hyderabad. This place is famous for its forts and sanctuaries and was once under the rule of the Kakatiyas, Musunuri Nayaks and Velama kings, Reddy kings, Qutb Shahi, and the Nizams of Hyderabad. As a result, the place is a store of architectural wonders. So, visiting this place is one of the best things to do in Telangana. The place has derived its name from Stambadri, the Godavari River flows through the town. . Ideal duration is 1 Night/2 Days.Khammam Railway Station is the nearest railhead .Best time to visit is October and March.
