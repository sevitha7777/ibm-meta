Coorg

A quaint and not so popular hill station, Coorg is aptly called ‘Scotland of India’. Its surreal beauty makes it one of the most fascinating tourist places in Karnataka. Endowed with coffee estates full of aroma, luxuriant greenery dotting the mountains, and stunning waterfalls plunging down the misty mountains, Coorg charms each and every one of its visitors. Another unique attraction of Coorg is the Tibetan Monastery. Do not miss these when visited, Go trekking along the winding trails of the coffee & spice plantations, visit elephant camp, coracle rides in the river, wildlife sanctuaries, monasteries, and local Madikeri market for shopping, pick some best coffees, spices, and vanilla stick from Madikeri Bazaar. The ideal duration can be one day. The best time to visit the place is March to June. Coorg is about 106 km from Mangalore rail station and about 260 km from the Bangalore Airport. Mangalore Station & Hassan Station are also in close proximity.
