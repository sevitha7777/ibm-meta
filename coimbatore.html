Coimbatore

Often termed as "Manchester of South India", the second-largest city in Tamil Nadu,photographically situated on the banks of River Noyyal. Coimbatore, also known as "KOVAI", is a welcoming area with the backdrop of hustle-bustle and charm of activity and industry. From ancient temples to classy new buildings, from breathtaking mountains to jaw-dropping waterfalls, from mouth-watering cuisine to pleasant weather all year round, Coimbatore has everything you could possibly need to make for a perfect holiday experience. Coimbatore is home to a massive 112 feet tall statue of Lord Shiva, known as the Adiyogi Shiva Statue. Featured in the Guinness Book of World Records, this architectural masterpiece is a very popular tourist spot in Coimbatore and stands as a symbol of self-transformation and inner peace.
