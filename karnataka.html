The geography and landscape of Karnataka is such that it serves as an ideal destination for diverse and enthralling tourist locations and landmarks. Located in the midst of Western Ghats, Deccan Plateau and Kannad Coast, Karnataka is home to various forests, beaches, waterfalls, coffee plantations, lakes and all the bounties of nature. The state also boasts of historically significant monuments and structures that attract visitors from far and beyond. Also culturally diverse and ancient, Karnataka has a historic past and rich heritage for the travelers to explore.
