Banglore

Bangalore, the capital city is the most cosmopolitan city in India. It is a melting pot that is home to people from different backgrounds, cultures and skills. It is the hub of Information Technology that houses a huge number of Software, Hardware and telecom companies that provide services to clients around the world. It is also called the garden city. Bangalore has pleasant and comfortable weather round the year and so it is one of the best places to visit in Karnataka in summer. Full of wildlife, nature, ruins, museums, architectural wonders, and gardens, Bangalore is a treat for tourists and travelers. Some of the things you should not miss include Taking a Vineyard tour, Microlight Flying, Paintballing, Go-karting, Exploring Snow City, Visiting Amusement park, Visiting shopping malls, try local food. The ideal duration may be a week. The best time to visit the place is October to February. The best way to travel to this city is either by train to Bangalore Railway station or by air to Bengaluru International airport.
