Jodhpur

Embellished with innumerable imprints of chivalry, architectural prowess, and natural beauty, Jodhpur tourist places invite you to behold the charisma of the past. Founded in 1459 A.D. by Rao Jodha, the city treats its tourists with the grandeur of the past by offering them several options for sightseeing in Jodhpur. If you are passionate about enjoying a sneak peek into the bygone era, visit Jodhpur. Amongst much-preferred places to visit in Jodhpur are palaces, forts, shrines, museums, and gardens that articulate the erstwhile excellence.
